Participating in the MIT Fintech Integrator was an exploration into how artificial intelligence can be used to predict market volatility and automate complex decision-making processes in trading environments.

The project focused on building a "signal-to-noise" filter that could aggregate disparate data sources and provide actionable insights for institutional investors. This reinforced the idea that in the future of finance, the winner is whoever has the best information processing system.
