Organizing the Basketball Tournament was a lesson in logistical coordination and community engagement. Beyond the scores, the goal was to create a platform for inter-departmental bonding through shared physical challenges.

Key Outcomes:
- Managed scheduling and venue logistics for over 10 teams.
- Coordinated with campus facilities and external vendors for equipment.
- Fostered a competitive yet collaborative environment that bridged diverse student backgrounds.
