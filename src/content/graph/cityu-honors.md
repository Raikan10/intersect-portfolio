Graduating with First Class Honours from City University of Hong Kong represents a rigorous multi-disciplinary journey. The curriculum balanced technical depth in Computer and Data Engineering with the strategic lens of Business Economics.

Key Focus Areas:
- Systems Architecture and VLSI Design
- Data Structures and Algorithmic Complexity
- Econometric Modeling and Market Dynamics

This foundation provided the technical rigors necessary to approach complex systems from both a hardware efficiency and an economic utility perspective.
