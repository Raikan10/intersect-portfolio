SLEEEP Technologies is a study in spatial optimization. As part of the engineering team, the challenge was to create high-density living and resting modular systems that integrated smart hardware with a seamless digital user experience.

This role required a convergence of physical product design and digital control systems. It reinforced the belief that the best technology is invisible, facilitating human needs (like rest and recovery) through precise, automated environmental control.
