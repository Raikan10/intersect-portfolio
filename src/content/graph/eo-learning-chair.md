In the role of Learning Chair for the Entrepreneurs' Organization (EO) Future Leaders, the mission was to curate a curriculum of high-impact knowledge.

Focus Areas:
- Designing "learning journeys" for high-growth founders.
- Facilitating mastermind groups focused on technical scaling.
- Sourcing industry experts for deep-dive workshops.

Learning is a network effect. By creating better nodes of information, we elevate the entire local ecosystem of builders.
