As Sports Director, the focus was on the logistics of athleticism. Managing multiple sporting divisions requires a system of coordination that balances seasonal scheduling with individual athlete development.

Leadership Achievements:
- Managed a budget for collegiate-level athletic equipment.
- Overhauled the inter-departmental league structure.
- Championed the integration of wellness metrics into team selection.

Sport is the ultimate test of a decentralized system—where individual agents (players) must operate autonomously within a shared strategy.
