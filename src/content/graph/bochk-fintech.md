The BOCHK Fintech Hackathon was a high-stakes arena to test the viability of decentralized finance in a traditional banking context. Our team developed a prototype for friction-less cross-border settlements that integrated real-time identity verification.

The experience emphasized the need for "regulatory-friendly" innovation—designing systems that are both revolutionary in their decentralization and compliant with existing financial frameworks.
