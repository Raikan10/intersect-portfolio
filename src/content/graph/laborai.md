Laborai is a project born out of the need for more intelligent workforce management. Instead of treating labor as a static resource, Laborai uses predictive modeling to match talent with task demand in real-time.

Features:
- Dynamic shift scheduling based on employee wellbeing metrics.
- Skill-based task routing.
- Real-time productivity analytics without intrusive surveillance.

This project sits at the intersection of AI and Human Resources, aiming to make work more efficient and more humane.
