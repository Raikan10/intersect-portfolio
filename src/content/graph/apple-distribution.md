Collaborating within the Apple Distribution network provided insights into one of the most sophisticated supply chains in the world. The work focused on process optimization and the data systems that ensure precision delivery at a global scale.

Understanding the "logistics of excellence" at a company like Apple reinforces the importance of detail-oriented systems design. Every point of the distribution loop—from factory output to end-user delivery—is a node that can be optimized for greater efficiency and user delight.
