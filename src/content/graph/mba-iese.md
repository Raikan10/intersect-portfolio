The IESE MBA journey is a deep dive into the "human side" of strategy. While engineering provides the "how," the MBA provides the "why." The program focuses on the Case Method, forcing decisions in high-ambiguity environments.

Key Learnings:
- Behavioral Decision Theory
- Financial Structuring and Venture Capital
- Global Macroeconomics and Market Entry

This period represents the synthesis of technical engineering rigors with the strategic foresight required to lead large-scale organizations.
