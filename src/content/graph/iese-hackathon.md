The IESE Hackathon was an exercise in bridging the gap between technical execution and business scalability. Unlike traditional engineering hackathons, the focus here was on ensuring that the technological solution directly addressed a high-value business problem with a viable path to market.

This role involved managing corporate partners, mentoring teams on their value proposition, and ensuring that the final presentations combined technical rigor with strategic clarity.
