The Nothing Hackathon was a challenge in restraint. The goal was to create a digital experience that aligned with Nothing's hardware philosophy: technology that gets out of the way.

We focused on haptic-driven navigation and "ambient" notifications that allowed users to stay informed without being distracted. It was a study in human-computer interaction (HCI) and the importance of sensory-first design.
