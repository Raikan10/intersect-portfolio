The Electronic Engineering Quiz Competition was designed to foster a spirit of healthy academic rivalry while reinforcing core technical concepts. 

As an organizer under the EE Honors Society, the challenge was to curate a balanced set of problems—ranging from signal processing mathematics to circuit design practicalities—that would engage students across all undergraduate years. It served as a key community-building pillar for the department.
