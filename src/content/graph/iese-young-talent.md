The Young Talent Path (YTP) was the initial gateway into the IESE ecosystem. It served as an early introduction to the school's philosophy of "People-Centered Management."

Through various workshops and networking intensives, the YTP focused on building the interpersonal foundations necessary for global leadership. It highlighted the importance of ethical decision-making and long-term value creation over short-term optimization.
